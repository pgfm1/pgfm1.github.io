Selection of hobbies that I feel like writing about and sharing. I may add more in the future.

cooking

I started cooking because I wanted to enjoy what I made if I had to feed myself, and this quickly escalated into a large amount of learning and refinement. Cooking is now a hobby which I casually experiment with on at least a weekly basis.

martial arts

I've been training Shōrin-ryū Karate and a line of Matayoshi Kobudo for years. In addition to regular classes I train at home, take extensive notes, and have started building and investing in some equipment. I'm unlikely to discuss my direct training here, but I may discuss things I build, strength and conditioning training, philosophy, and other such things.

arcade

Arcade is a relatively new hobby for me (2020). I've been particularly absorbed with shmups and the design of arcade sticks in general. While I also fiddle with beat-em-ups and platformers, I've revived a love for shmups that started long, long ago. I don't have nearly as much time as I'd like for gaming, but I do keep at it and I'm also working on designing my own equipment.

jrpgs

I love old jrpgs and am slowly working through a list. One of my favorite games (and greatest time investments) is Final Fantasy 9, but this list mostly deviates heavily from that. I'm exploring the genre with some friends and will share the experience here.

bread

In 2019 a friend of mine gave me a jar of sourdough starter and some basic references, and I joined them in a quest to make bread. I now bake bread weekly, experiment frequently, share notes, and have greatly expanded my bread repertoire.
